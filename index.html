Midus Trade GmbH Review – Is midustrade.com a Scam?

Introduction

Midus Trade GmbH, operating via midustrade.com, claims to be a digital asset broker with over 30 years of experience. However, a closer look at the broker reveals major inconsistencies and potential red flags.

Website Registration and False Claims

The domain midustrade.com was registered on February 23, 2025, making it a very new broker. Despite this, the website falsely claims to have been in operation for 30 years and to have launched the "Midus Trading Platform" in 2009 – a blatant lie since the domain only appeared recently.

Company Details – Fake Office Locations

Midus Trade GmbH provides addresses in Poland and the UK, suggesting they have offices there. However, when investigating the company, we found that Midus Trade GmbH is registered in Switzerland as a healthcare facility provider, with no connection to financial services or trading.

Trading Conditions and Account Types

Midus Trade GmbH offers several account types with a high minimum deposit of $2,500. The differences between these accounts lie in fees and available instruments. Upgrading to a higher-tier account requires a deposit of at least $10,000, a risky proposition for traders.

Licensing and Regulatory Status

Midus Trade GmbH is not licensed by any financial regulatory authority. This lack of regulation means there is no investor protection, making it a highly risky platform for traders.

No User Reviews or Reputation

There are no user reviews of midustrade.com available on the internet. This is highly unusual for a broker that claims to have been in operation for decades. The lack of feedback suggests the broker is either brand new or deliberately suppressing negative reviews.

Conclusion – Is Midus Trade GmbH a Scam?

Based on our findings, Midus Trade GmbH (midustrade.com) exhibits multiple red flags, including:

  • False claims about its history and platform launch date.
  • Providing fake office locations.
  • Being unlicensed and unregulated.
  • Requiring high deposits with no verified track record.

It is highly likely that midustrade.com is a scam. We advise traders to avoid this broker and seek regulated alternatives.
